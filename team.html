Our Founder & CEO

Nnaemeka Buhari

NNAEMEKA BUHARI is a teacher, capacity development expert, speaker and thought leader with over ten years' experience in the Education sector with expertise in O'level and A'level Chemistry, capacity development and use of technology in education for teachers and students.

He possesses an O.N.D and B.TECH (Hons.) in Chemical Engineering, PGDE in Science Education and M.Ed (in view) in Education Planning and Administration. Olorunfemi is a Google Certified Educator (Level 1), a Certified Microsoft Innovative Educator, a registered member of the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria (TCRN) and also a member of the Microsoft Educators community and a WAEC Examiner in Chemistry.
