About our screening tasks


As part of every application, we ask applicants to complete a screening task.


The task is intended to give you a sense of the work you’ll be doing if you join our team and to give us a sense of how you work and think. We view this as an opportunity for you to demonstrate how you tackle big problems – if you don’t have all the details worked out, that’s OK! Show us your approach – where will you start, how will you measure whether or not you’re successful?


The screening task is solely for screening and interview purposes – we won’t use any materials you develop.
