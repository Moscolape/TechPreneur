Empower lifelong learning at work and in the community


Our technology entrepreneurship program helps girls around the world develop their problem solving skills as well as confidence in their own leadership abilities. Technovation alumnae go on to work at Fortune 500 companies, speak at the UN, and join UNESCO summits. The adults who help them along the way also grow in incredible ways.


Get involved and support young women who are ready to build a better world. Engage employees as mentors and judges, become a funding partner, or help Technovation alumnae find their career footing.



Technovation is Global


When you volunteer with Technovation your impact is felt around the world.

Girls from 100+ countries have built technology solutions to solve problems in their communities. Volunteering with Technovation means having the opportunity to support and learn alongside these incredible young women.

Through Technovation:

  • Girls create their own tech solutions to community problems—and along the way become confident community leaders and change-makers.
  • Mentors become more confident, creative, and report having a better relationship with their company after volunteering.

Engage employees as volunteer mentors and judges globally


Help close the gender gap in tech.
Engage your employees as mentors to support girls as they learn about coding, AI, and business, or as judges who provide feedback on the tech prototypes girls build to solve community problems. Mentors and judges have the opportunity to work with girls around the world.


Our corporate engagement team will be with you every step of the way to ensure your employees have a successful and meaningful volunteer experience with Technovation.
